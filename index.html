About

Data Updated:

This tool aims to track numerous player's statistical averages over the course of a season. For each statistic, this data is plotted against the benchmark of the past 30 NBA MVPs. Additional players can be added to the graph for comparison using the Add Player/Remove Player controls. The MVP line can be refined to view only backcourt MVPs or only frontcourt MVPs, from the past 30 years. Additionally, advanced season stat points are plotted and can be viewed by switching to the "Adv Season Stats" tab.
